Our Vision

Users

Casa is a mobile platform that allows users to explore, request, and use household services. These include spaces such as kitchens and bedrooms as well as amenities like printers and parking. Users get access to high quality amenities and spaces at an affordable price.

Renters

Most of your house goes unused for a large portion of the day. Our application allows people to profit off of services that are sitting idle. Our simplistic UI/UX also allows users to quickly become renters and instantly begin monetizing their homes.
