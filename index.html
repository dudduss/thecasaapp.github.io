Amenities and Spaces Exactly When You Need Them

Search

Search for anything you need that a house can offer (ie. A bedroom for the night, parking, a washer/dryer etc.) Filter your results based on price, availability and distance.

Request

Request a service by specifying a time you would like to use a found listing. Wait for the renter to confirm the request. Enjoy the room or ammenity!

Enjoy

Once you use enter the renter's house and use the space/ammenity, confirm the transaction by signing into the house on the app. Make sure you leave a rating and review!

Monetize Existing Infrastructure

Create Profile

Create a renter profile by inputting your house location and your payment information.

Customize Casa

Input services your house can provide and certain fields about each listing you select (ie. price, availability and description).

Make Money

Accept requests to use your space/amenities. Make sure users sign-in to your house on entry. Enjoy the cash flow!

Our Vision

Users

Casa is a mobile platform that allows users to explore, request, and use household services. These include spaces such as kitchens and bedrooms as well as amenities like printers and parking. Users get access to high quality amenities and spaces at an affordable price.

Renters

Most of your house goes unused for a large portion of the day. Our application allows people to profit off of services in one's home that are sitting idle. Our simplistic UI/UX also allows users to quickly become renters and instantly begin monetizing their homes.
